# Homepage CTA Design Notes

Source: user-provided screenshot of the Mamiviet homepage welcome CTA.

- Background: dark warm brown/charcoal, approximately `#2c241c`.
- Brand accent: muted bronze, approximately `#b48c67`, already present as Tailwind `primary`.
- Typography: uppercase, compact sans serif, high letter spacing.
- Mood target: European refined, restrained, simple, high-contrast enough to stand out without using saturated colors.
- CTA direction: replace disconnected corner brackets with a single fine bronze border, subtle dark fill, delicate inner line, and calm bronze hover state.
